The Core Problem: Why Predictions Fail

1. Sequence Bias

Models ignore epigenetic landscape; cell-specific context is ignored.

2. Uncertainty

Point estimates lead to risky clinical decisions.

3. Data Leakage

Inflated metrics due to gene similarity across splits.

4. Lab Bottleneck

High failure rate slows validation cycles.

S3: Methodology - Module 1 Page 12

Quantifying Risk: Beta & Conformal Prediction

Beta Regression

Predicts μ (mean efficacy) and φ (precision/certainty). Best for 0-1 bounded efficacy.

Conformal Logic

Provides 90% coverage guarantees: Real-world error control for clinical safety.

Clinical Decision Support: Rejection of high-uncertainty guides.
